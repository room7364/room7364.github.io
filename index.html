Apple pie recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 7 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 resipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • 4 tablespoons milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees C
  2. Mix together the sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg and lemon peel
  3. Line one crust in a 9-inch deep-dish pie pan. Layer 1.3 of apples into pie crust. Sprinkle with sugar mixture and repeat until done. Sprinkle with lemon juice and dot with butter
  4. Place second pie crust on top of filling and flute the adges. Cut vents in top crust and brush with milf for a glazed apperance if desired
  5. Bake at 220 degrees C for 40 to 50 minutes
